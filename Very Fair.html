Very Fair Chinese Restaurant

Restaurant Info:

Address: 4002 Sheppard Ave E, Scarborough, ON M1S 1S6

Hours: 10am - 11pm

Phone: (416)-332-1861


Our Review

This restaurant serves good food at fair prices. The servers are nice except when they're in the back kitchen they are yelling at each other. The dishes are around $3. The food is decent, it tastes good and looks aesthetically pleasing. The dish Spencer enjoyed the most was the radish cake, it had a nice texture and a good taste. The service is very fair, with most of their utensils being chipped and or dirty. The restaurant is located in the basement of the mall and at lunches, there iss no line up. We do not recommend coming to this restaurant unless you are in the area.

Spencer's Food Reviews rates this restauraunt 3.5 stars
