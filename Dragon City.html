Dragon City

Restaurant Info:

Address: 280 Spadina Ave, Toronto, ON M5T 3A5

Phone: (416)-408-4999

Hours: 9am - 10:30pm

Our Review:

Dragon City is in the heart of Chinatown, located on the 3rd floor of Dragon City Mall at Spadina and Dundas. This restaurant is very spacious. The food at Dragon City is a hit or a miss, sometimes they have great food and sometimes the food is bad. The food looked very sloppy. We went to review the restaurant on March 30th, 2019. The food tasted decent but the looks of the food were bad. It looked like no effort was put into it. I had the Donut Rice roll and it looked and tasted horrible. The rice roll was too thick and mushy ruining the taste. The food is served by carts. The service is below average as the staff choose to serve people that go there every day. If you do not go often you probably will get the worst seats. The worse seats are in the corner of the restaurants as it is the last place the carts will go leaving you with bad food options. The dishes are fairly clean. However, some of table cloths and chairs may be dirty and make sure to avoid using the washrooms. The restaurant is open every day even on holidays.

Spencer's Food Reviews rates this restaurant 3 stars.

Overall we do not recommend going to this restaurant if it is your first time going to Dim Sum. The food quality and service is not the greatest and there are better restaurant options in Chinatown.
