Dim Sum King


Restaurant Info:

Address: 421 Dundas St W, Toronto, ON M5T 2W4

Phone: (416)-551-3366

Hours: 9am - 10pm

Check Out Their Website


Our Review:

The Dim Sum King is a great dim sum restaurant located in Chinatown. The food is served in cool carts, and the restaurant is very spacious and clean. Most of the food is very good. Spencer's favourite dim sum dishes here is the sticky rice wrapped in lotus leaf, the steamed pork and shrimp dumplings. The prices are pretty cheap ranging from $3-5. The staff are very friendly, helpful and efficient. The weekends tend to be busy but there is always room and the wait times are short. We recommend coming here if you are in Chinatown looking for a good quality meal that's cheap and quick.

Spencer's Food Reviews rates this restaurant 4.2 Stars
