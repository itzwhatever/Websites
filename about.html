The History Of Spencer's Food Reviews

Spencer's Food Reviews has been running since 2014. What started as a project at school became the blog you know today. Spencer's school project was really successful and with the help of some teachers, "The Restaurant of The Month" segment of the project was featured in the school newspaper. Subsequent, a parent approached Spencer and him to become a writer for the Toronto Star. The Toronto Star gave Spencer's Food Review a platform. From there, more opportunities emerged. In 2018, Katrina became the co-owner of Spencer's Food Reviews. The co-owners decided to create a website so they can constantly update their followers with content. The blog became a success and we hope to continue to provide our followers with new content every week.

Frequently Asked Quesions

Q: Why are Dim Sum dishes in this container when it is served?
A: It's a bamboo steamer. It keeps the food warm.

Q: How often do you post new content?
A: We post new content every week.

Q: Why is your blog mainly about Chinese food?
A: Katrina and Spencer are both Chinese. They have been to many Chinese restaurants and they enjoy Chinese food. The website teaches people about Chinese food and they recommend places to those who have not have Dim Sum before.

Q: When will you start reviewing foods of other cultures?
A: Soon! Once we complete our Chinese Food Series, we will review more!

Q: Why is this name Spencer's Food Review?
A: Spencer was the one who founded and created the blog.
