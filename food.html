Dim Sum Dishes

Baozi - 包子

Baozi's are Chinese buns. There are many diffrent types of buns served at Dim Sum. The buns are freshly made and have many flavours. The buns are really fluffy and have many enriched fillings.
Read More


Dumplings - 饺子

Dumplings is a very popular dish in China. Dumplings made in Dim Sum are very unique. They are not your average homemade dumpling. The dumplings at Dim Sum are made in a variety of ways and have many fillings to chose from. All the dumplings are freshly made and are very tasty. Learn more about the different dumplings here!
Read More


Sticky Rice Wrapped in Lotus Leaf-糯米鸡

The Sticky Rice Wrapped in Lotus is a classic dish. This dish is made differently depending on the restaurant. The portion of this dish also varies as it can be very small or large. The rice has many fillings. Learn more about the fillings in the sticky rice and how it is made here.
Read More


Ribs-骨

Ribs are another classic dish at Dim Sum. Did you know there are two types of ribs served at Dim Sum? There are pork ribs and beef ribs. They are both very similar but they have many differences aswell.
Read More


Rice Noodle Rolls 肠粉

The Rice Noodle Rolls are very delicious. They are made from thin sheets of rice noodles and then it is rolled up to make a roll. This dish has three main fillings, shrimp, beef or a fried donut roll. This dish is usually served with dipping sauce such as soy sauce.
Read More


Turnip Cake-蘿蔔糕

Turnip Cake is a less popular dish but is one of our favourites. This dish is pan-fried and is cut up to rectangles to serve. It is both crunchy and soft. There are other substitutions to make Turnip Cake if you are allergic. The other flavours are equally as tasty and they are all served all Dim Sum.
