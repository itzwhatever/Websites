Rice Noodle Rolls - 肠粉


There are a variety of rice rolls out there. Rice rolls are a steamed dish and can be made with many different fillings. At Dim Sum restaurants they serve shrimp rolls, beef rolls, deep fried doughnut roll, barbeque pork and plain rice rolls. The rice rolls are served with a sweet diluted soy sauce. The deep fried doughnut rolls are served with a thick soy sauce and peanut sauce. In this post, we will talk about the three main types of rice rolls.


Shrimp Rice Roll - 蝦腸

This is a very popular dish found at all Dim Sum restaurants. This roll have a shrimp filling and is served with soy sauce.

Beef Rice Roll - 牛肉腸

The beef roll has a filling of beef with finely diced vegetables like scallions. This dish is also very popular and is found at every Dim Sum restaurant.

Fried Doughnut Rice roll - 炸兩

This is one of Katrina's favourite dish. The rice noodle is smooth and the doughnut is crunchy and soft. This rice roll is served with peanut butter and soy sauce.

This is a video taken in China showing how the rice rolls are made The rice noodle is spread thinly on a pan sheet and then is quickly rolled.
