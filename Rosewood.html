Rosewood Asian Cuisine

Restaurant Info:

Address: 463 Dundas St W, Toronto, ON M5T 1G8

Hours: 9am - 2pm

Phone: (416)-332-1861

Our Review:

Rosewood has continued to expand and renovate its restaurant for the past 5 years. Rosewood offers both all you can eat sushi as well as dim sum. Most of the food is expensive after lunch hours, but the food is really good. Spencers favourite dish is the BBQ pork buns because they are puffy and have a nice taste to them. He also likes the pork and shrimp dumplings which are also made flawlessly. There are a lot of people who dine in but the wait isn't too long. This is one of the more fancier restaurants in Chinatown. We recommend coming here if you like good quality dim sum at a higher price.

Spencer's Food Review rates this restauraunt 4 stars
