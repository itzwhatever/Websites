Sticky Rice Wrapped in Lotus Leaf - 糯米雞

The Sticky Rice Wrapped in Lotus Leaf is also called Lo Mai Gai which translates to pearl chicken. The rice is not white rice but it is sticky rice. The rice has filling in the middle. Stuffing can vary from restaurant, but traditionally it is stuffed with an egg yolk, marinated pork, chicken, beans, a Chinese sausage and Chinese mushrooms. The filling may vary depending on the restaurant. When eating make sure to unwrap the lotus wrap. Do not eat the leaf because it is just a garnish so make sure to unwrap the leaf from the rice.
